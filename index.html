About

Comment sentiment analysis is a specific form of sentiment analysis that focuses on extracting sentiments from comments left by users on online platforms. This type of analysis can be used to understand customer satisfaction, identify trends, and track brand reputation. The objective of this research is to develop robust methodologies and models that effectively analyze and interpret sentiments expressed in user-generated comments across various online platforms. By applying advanced natural language processing techniques, we will be able to extract meaningful insights from this unstructured data that can be used to improve business performance
